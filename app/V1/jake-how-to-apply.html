How to apply

Before you apply, you will need your:

  • Activity Credit Referral Number
  • National Insurance Number

You will also be asked to provide proof of your annual income. You could upload:

  • your most recent bank statement
  • the last payslip you recieved
  • your p60 or p45
  • your SA302 (if you're self-employed)

To complete your application, you will also need to provide information about:

  • any benefits that you may claim
  • your name
  • your date of birth
  • where we can contact you
