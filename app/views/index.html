Designing a service to encourage exercise in return for cash.

Hypothesis

We believe that there is low activity levels from people of low income, which is leading to an increase in obesity related health issues. We think this is because people are struggling to find the motivation for regular exercise. So, if we can make exercise more accessible and valuable through providing additional income in return for physical activity, we will see an increase in peoples motivation to maintain a regular exercise lifestyle.
