Designing a service to encourage exercise in return for cash.

Hypothesis

We believe that there is an increase in people not exercising which is leading to an increase in obesity related health issues. We think this is because people are struggling to find the motivation for regular exercise. So, if we can make exercise more accessible and valuable providing rewards in return for physical activity, we will see an increase in peoples motivation to maintain a regular exercise lifestyle.
