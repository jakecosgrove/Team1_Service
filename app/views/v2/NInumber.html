What is your National Insurance number?

It’s on your National Insurance card, benefit letter, payslip or P60. For example, ‘QQ 12 34 56 C’.
I cannot find my National Insurance number
You can find your National Insurance number on your payslip, P60, or letters about tax, pensions and benefits. If you cannot find it you can ask for a reminder through post.
