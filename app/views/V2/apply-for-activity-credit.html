Apply for Activity Credit

You must be eligible to apply for Activity Credit. You will be eligible if you:

  • live in the UK
  • earn less than £16,000 per year
  • have an Activity Credit Referral number from your doctor or other approved healthcare provider

Before you apply, you will need your:

  • Activity Credit Referral number
  • National insurance number

You will also be asked for your name and address.

To complete your application, you will also need to tell us how we can contact you. We will update you on the progress of your application. We also may need to request more information about your application.
